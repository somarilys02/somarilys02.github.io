Fluffernutter Muddy Buddies

I was absolutely obsessed with Muddy Buddies growing up, but this version takes it to the next level. They’ve got all the nostalgic flavors I loved as a kid, with a fluffernutter twist thanks to peanut butter and marshmallow fluff. Sweet, crunchy, and completely addictive—you’ll want to make a big batch because they disappear fast.

Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 5 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes Yields: 8 cups

Ingredients

  • 6 1/2 cups rice chex cereal
  • 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar

COATING:

  • 3/4 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup marshmallow fluff
  • pinch of salt

OPTIONAL MIX-INS:

  • mini marshmallows
  • mini peanut butter cups
  • mini chocolate chips
  • peanut butter chips
  • reese’s Pieces

Instructions

  1. Add the chex cereal to a large mixing bowl and set aside.
  2. In a medium pot over low heat, add the chocolate chips, peanut butter, butter, marshmallow fluff, vanilla extract, and salt. Stir continuously with a rubber spatula until smooth and fully melted, about 5–7 minutes.
  3. Pour the mixture over the Rice Chex and very gently fold with the rubber spatula until all the cereal is evenly coated.
  4. Allow the coated cereal to cool for about 4 to 5 minutes so it’s no longer piping hot, but not fully hardened.
  5. Transfer to a large zip-top bag or container, add the powdered sugar (adding as much or as little as you like), seal, and shake until the cereal is well coated.
  6. Optional: Spread onto a baking sheet to finish cooling and setting. This isn’t necessary, but it can help the mix stay extra crispy.
  7. Once cooled, mix in any of the optional add-ins like mini marshmallows, peanut butter cups, or Reese’s Pieces or just leave as is like the original!
  8. Store in an airtight container or ziploc back at room temperature for up to 1 week.
