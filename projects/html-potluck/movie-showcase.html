Avatar The Way of Water

Runtime: 3 Hrs 12 Min Genre: Action/Drama

Synopsis

Jake Sully, Neytiri, and their family is being forced to flee their forest home and seek refuge with the Metkayina, a Water Na'vi clan, when the RDA returns to Pandora for a new invasion. The family must learn to adapt to the new, aquatic environment and lifestyle while being relentlessly hunted by Colonel Quaritch, who has returned in an avatar body to seek revenge.
